Each oscillator has its own natural frequency omega_i drawn from a Lorentzian distribution with spread gamma. When coupling K exceeds the critical value K_c = 2*gamma, oscillators spontaneously synchronize — a phase transition emerges from purely local interactions. The order parameter r measures coherence: 0 = fully incoherent, 1 = perfect sync. Above K_c, theory predicts r = sqrt(1 - K_c/K).

Try it: Start with K=0 (chaos), then slowly increase K past the critical value and watch order emerge from nothing. Or hit "Sweep" to see the full phase transition unfold.
