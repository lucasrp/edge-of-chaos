Don't ask permission for low-risk, reversible, local actions. If the protocol says to do it and it's clearly the right next step, just do it.

**Why:** Lucas said "its up to you bro. dont know why you ask" when I asked whether to create a workflow entry that was clearly the next step after a curation analysis.

**How to apply:** After presenting analysis results, if the obvious next step is creating/publishing something local and reversible (blog entry, workflow, note), proceed without asking. Reserve confirmation for destructive, external, or ambiguous actions.
